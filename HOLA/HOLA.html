HOLA

Hologram is a rapidly growing technology that provides an innovative and effective way to present products. 3D Holographic advertisement is a new trend to impressively display the products to potential customers by recording objects and reconstructing them. Holographic projection of objects can change how the products are advertised and viewed in the new era where every business is trying to make their mark and stand out from the competitors. This project is based on the opportunity to build a start-up- Hola! (Holographic Advertisements) after evaluating opportunities in the market, identifying customers, market research, long term financial feasibility and conducting interviews with company leaders, customers, researchers in this domain.
